!!Con West 2020

the joy, excitement, and surprise of computing

Feb 29-March 1, 2020; Santa Cruz, CA

  • Home

!!Con (pronounced “bang bang con”) West is a two-day conference of ten-minute talks about the joy, excitement, and surprise of computing, and the west-coast successor to !!Con! It will be held in Santa Cruz, California, on the campus of UC Santa Cruz, on February 29-March 1, 2020!

!!Con West is a pay-what-you-want conference, with tickets that will be available soon!

Speakers

Our call for talk proposals is now available.

What was the schedule of the event?

The rough schedule looks like this for both days:

  • Registration opens at 9am. Continental breakfast will be available.
  • First talk starts at 10:30am.
  • Boxed lunch will be served around 12:30pm.
  • Wrap-up will end by 5pm.

The detailed program has more information.
