!!Con (pronounced “bang bang con”) West is a two-day conference of ten-minute talks about the joy, excitement, and surprise of computing, and the west-coast successor to !!Con! Join us in Santa Cruz, California, on the campus of UC Santa Cruz, on February 23-24, 2019!

!!Con West is a pay-what-you-want conference, but tickets are now sold out. But if you missed the registration, don’t fret: we still encourage you to get on the ticket waitlist!

!!Con West will also be livestreamed and recorded, speaker permitting!

Who’s speaking?

Two amazing keynote speakers (Lynn Cyrin and VJ Um Amel), and 30 other amazing speakers! We’re incredibly excited by this year’s talk line-up, and we know that you will be too – check it out!

Our call for talk proposals is now closed. Thanks to everyone who submitted one!

How do I get updates?

Follow us on Twitter at @bangbangconwest!

You can also subscribe to our mailing list! We send roughly five emails a year, and we’ll never share your email address with anyone else.
