Sponsor !!Con West!

!!Con is a tremendous amount of fun, and we think that it brings a lot of value to its attendees, and to the programming community at large. But !!Con also costs money to run, and we’re committed to making sure that it remains affordable to everybody who wants to participate.

As such, we need your help to keep !!Con West open and available to all. In exchange, we’d love to help put your company in front of our 200+ smart, creative, thoughtful participants. Several sponsorship levels are available. If you have any questions, please contact us.
